Our Comprehensive Medical & Critical Care Services

Specializing in **Critical Care Management** alongside expert treatment for common and complex medical conditions.

24/7 Intensive & Critical Care Unit (ICU)

Our core focus: Immediate and advanced management of **Cardiac Arrests**, severe respiratory failure, trauma, and post-operative critical states.

Cardiovascular & Hypertension Management

Comprehensive diagnosis and long-term treatment for **Hypertension**, **Heart-Related Issues**, including ECG and stabilization protocols.

Diabetes & Metabolic Disorder Management

Dedicated clinical care for **Diabetic Management**, including blood sugar stabilization, complication prevention, and patient education.

Stroke & Paralysis Rehabilitation Support

Acute management and initial care for **Paralysis** (Stroke/CVA), focusing on stabilization and early referral for physical rehabilitation.

Asthma & General Respiratory Management

Diagnosis and acute treatment for conditions like **Asthma** and Chronic Obstructive Pulmonary Disease (COPD), including nebulization and oxygen therapy.

General Medicine Consultation & Care

Full spectrum of general medical services, fever management, infectious disease diagnosis, and chronic illness follow-up.
