About Vithai Hospital

Vithai Hospital and Critical Care Centre is a premier healthcare facility dedicated to providing comprehensive medical services with a focus on critical care. Located in the heart of Loha, we serve our community with state-of-the-art medical facilities and compassionate care.

Our mission is to deliver exceptional healthcare services that combine clinical excellence with patient-centered care. We believe in treating each patient as a valued individual, ensuring they receive personalized attention and the highest standard of medical treatment.

Our Facilities

Vithai Hospital is equipped with modern medical facilities to ensure the best possible care for our patients. We pride ourselves on maintaining high standards of medical infrastructure and patient safety.

ðŸ¥ 24/7 Intensive Care Unit (ICU)

Our state-of-the-art ICU is the cornerstone of our critical care services, operating around the clock to provide life-saving care when you need it most.

  • Round-the-Clock Availability: Open 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, including all holidays
  • Advanced Monitoring Systems: Continuous cardiac, respiratory, and vital signs monitoring with multi-parameter monitors
  • Ventilator Support: Modern ventilators for respiratory support and mechanical ventilation
  • Dedicated Critical Care Team: Trained intensivists, critical care nurses, and support staff available at all times
  • Immediate Emergency Response: Rapid response protocols for critical situations
  • Patient Safety Protocols: Strict infection control measures and hygiene standards
  • Family Support: Regular updates and counseling for patient families during ICU stay
  • Post-ICU Care: Comprehensive recovery and rehabilitation guidance

Our emergency department is always prepared to handle acute medical situations with swift and effective interventions.
